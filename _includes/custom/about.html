VINCI is a research institute at SFU focusing on Visual and INteractive Computing

Our members cover a wide range of research areas, including: - VC : Visual Computing (graphics, vision, AI/ML, mathematical modeling, etc.) - IC : Interactive Computing (HCI, visualization, human-centred design, etc.) - SI : Social Implications (digital media, ethics, policy, philosophy, governance, etc.) - DA : Domain-Specific Applications (languages, health, music, learning, business, history, etc.)
