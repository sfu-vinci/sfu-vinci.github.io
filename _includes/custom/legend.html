The institute members cover a wide range of research areas, including:
  • V : Visual Computing: graphics, vision, etc.
  • I : Interactive Computing: HCI, visualization, human-centred design, etc.
  • N : INtelligent Computing: AI, ML, robotics, mathematical modeling, etc.
  • S : Social Implications: digital media, ethics, policy, philosophy, governance, etc.
  • A : Domain-specific Applications: languages, health, music, learning, business, history, etc.
